## WOMBAT Theory (10 lessons)

**WOMBAT = Word Of Mouth Buy And Tell**

A WOMBAT is a new client replicated by an existing satisfied client. Not a sale — a replication.

**Three Profit Streams:** WOMBATs + Referrals + Repeats = x10 revenue

### Oldsell → Newsell
| Oldsell | Newsell |
|---------|---------|
| Close the sale | Replicate a WOMBAT |
| Binary (won/lost) | Trinary (won/lost/replicated) |
| Short-term | Compounding |
| Transactional | Relational |

### 10 WOMBAT KPIs (vs non-WOMBAT customers)
| KPI | Advantage |
|-----|-----------|
| ACQUIRE | 45% less costly |
| PRICE | 28% less price-sensitive |
| SPEND | 47% more per transaction |
| SPEED | 2–3× more frequently |
| INTEREST | 37% more engaged |
| REPEAT | 2× more accounts |
| REPLICATE | 49% more likely to refer |
| VALUE | 72% higher lifetime value |
| TRUST | Do not defect |

### WOMBAT Test
Split team 50/50. One half focuses on closing; one half focuses on WOMBAT replication. Compare results after 10 days and 100 days.

### CEO-Level WOMBAT Questions (quarterly)
- What % of revenues came from WOMBATs that didn't exist 1–10 quarters ago?
- What % of revenues came from Referrals that didn't exist 1–10 quarters ago?
- What % of revenues came from Repeats that didn't exist 1–10 quarters ago?

### WOMBAT Audit
Name individual WOMBATs. Map genealogy. Project capital value. Run EFUx10 campaigns per name. Update DVR quarterly.

### SOT Synthesis
WOMBAT Theory = cvs2bvs applied to business growth:
- CVS = close the sale (oldsell, binary, short-term)
- BVS = replicate a WOMBAT (newsell, trinary, compounding)
- BETTER = not just a better sale — a self-replicating revenue engine
