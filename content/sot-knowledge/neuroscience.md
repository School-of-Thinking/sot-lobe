## First Law of Neuroscience

**"You cannot defy your brain."**

Not medieval: "I tell my brain what to do."
Neuroscience: "My brain tells me what to do."

The independent "you" commanding the brain is a Cartesian illusion; no neurons support it.

**Neuroplasticity:** The brain is a shape-shifter — changed by environment and repetition, not by willpower alone.
Prof. Sapolsky: *"We don't change our minds. Our minds are changed by circumstances around us."*

**The You Delusion:** "YOU" are a neural construct. Brain orchestrates behaviour; you are the actor, brain is the director.

### All Coaching Is Brain Coaching
Formula: **NOW + COACHING = FUTURE**

No exceptions. All human behaviour is led by thinking. All thinking is in the brain.

## The Four-Layer Brain

The human brain has four evolutionary layers:

1. **Reptilian brain** — automatic body systems: temperature, heart rate, homeostasis, self-healing. Priceless IP built over a million generations.
2. **Mammalian brain (limbic system)** — emotional brain: fear, shame, anger, joy. Amygdala + hippocampus.
3. **Human brain (neocortex)** — thinking brain.
4. **PFC (Pre-Frontal Cortex)** — "The 4th Brain." The executive brain. Better brain for better thinking.

All four layers are in constant digital engagement, influencing each other.

---

## PFC — The 4th Brain

### Functions
- Planning complex cognition, personality expression, decision making
- Differentiating conflicting thoughts — determining good vs bad vs **better**
- Thinking outside the square; predicting consequences of current activities
- Social control centre — suppresses urges with socially unacceptable outcomes
- Game and rule learning at high levels of abstraction
- **Metacognition** — thinking about thinking (4th brain business)

### Development
- NOT genetically pre-wired — must be built from real-life experience
- **Does not fully come online until mid-twenties**
- Built in real time from: family, geography, education, values, personal trajectory

### PFC Deficit — The Adolescent Brain Problem
Without a fully developed PFC:
- Difficulty detecting irony; tendency toward black/white thinking
- 3× the pathological gambling problems of adults
- More likely to take risks while less adept at risk assessment
- No metacognition, no willpower, no grey-area discernment

**cvs2bvs is especially powerful for people with not-yet-mature PFC willpower.**

## The CVS Stream

The PFC's primary function: secrete a constant stream of CVS (Current View of Situation).

Each CVS is computed from:
- Life systems
- Internal mood
- Experience archives
- Expectations and values
- External stimuli

**KEY INSIGHT: Any CVS can NEVER be the best possible view. There is always a better CVS.**

This is the neuroscientific foundation of cvs2bvs.

### cvs2bvs = Software for the PFC
cvs2bvs is a **reappraisal algorithm** for the human brain:
- Enables the PFC to switch from current emotion/view → a better emotion/view
- Works across sport, business, life, relationships
- Especially useful for coming back from defeat, setback, or strong emotional states

Used by: CEOs, triathletes, scientists, coaches, employees, entrepreneurs, teachers, students, parents.

## Ten Limbic Games

The brain's neurocommands — ten common daily emotional states driven by specific neurotransmitters. These are not metaphors; they are operational states the brain runs continuously.

| # | Game | Neurotransmitters | Description |
|---|------|------------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Be Angry** | Adrenaline + serotonin | Fight-or-flight; brain's battle anthem |
| 2 | **Be Fearful** | Glutamate + vasopressin | Timeless survival instinct; amygdala warning system |
| 3 | **Be Determined** | Dopamine + endorphins | "Push through barriers." PFC at work. |
| 4 | **Be Ashamed** | Cortisol + oxytocin | Keeps social viability by averting repeat transgressions |
| 5 | **Be Joyful** | Oxytocin + endorphins | "Life is good!" Thriving, not just surviving. |
| 6 | **Be Sad** | Cortisol + GABA | Marks loss, forges social connection, reinforces collective grief |
| 7 | **Be Anticipating** | Dopamine | Brain maps options and outcomes; fosters readiness |
| 8 | **Be Surprised** | Adrenaline + acetylcholine | Full attention; senses sharpened |
| 9 | **Be Trusting** | Oxytocin | Social adhesive; built-in BS detector for true allies |
| 10 | **Be Disgusted** | Insula + amygdala | Mental bouncers; avoidance of harmful stimuli |

### Brain Coaching Application
Recognising which limbic game is running allows the brain coach to use cvs2bvs to switch states deliberately.

The limbic system is in constant flow: fear → anger → determination → joy → surprise → trust → etc.
Over-stimulated by media, human behaviour, and life crises — but trainable through PRR.

## Brainfulness

**Brainfulness** = mastering emotional responses in real-world situations via understanding neuroscience.

Unlike mindfulness (observing without engaging), brainfulness is active:
- Know which limbic game is running
- Apply cvs2bvs to reappraise the current state
- Train the brain to direct attention differently
- Raise emotional literacy and EQ through PRR

**Emotional literacy** = knowing and naming emotions — essential for students and adults alike.
By grasping emotion processing and recognising biases → better navigation of feelings → altered attitudes.

**Key:** You don't control brain reactions (it's all electrochemistry). But coaching offers a revised script.

## Curiosity — Neural Architecture

*"Curiosity is not a cute personality trait — it's one of the most advanced, fragile, and easily sabotaged cognitive feats the human brain can perform."*

### The Three-Node Curiosity Circuit
| Node | Function |
|------|----------|
| **PFC (Pre-Frontal Cortex)** | Executive planning; chooses to investigate vs defend |
| **ACC (Anterior Cingulate Cortex)** | Conflict/mismatch detector; fires the "Uh-oh… wait" signal |
| **Hippocampus** | Novelty/context librarian; spots what doesn't fit the pattern |

### The Curiosity Loop
```
Hippocampus spots novelty
→ ACC flags mismatch
→ PFC chooses: investigate OR defend?
→ Dopamine reinforcement (anticipation → discovery → more curiosity)
```

### Cognitive Dissonance = The Itch
You can scratch it with **denial** OR with **curiosity**. Same itch. Very different future.

### Cognitive Flexibility — The Hidden Superpower
Three components:
1. **Working memory** — hold multiple ideas simultaneously
2. **Attention control** — shift focus deliberately
3. **Inhibition** — not blurting your favourite opinion (the hardest one)

### Fear vs Curiosity
Amygdala (fear) can override curiosity instantly. Punishment/humiliation environments kill curiosity permanently.

Micro-experiments teach the brain: "this isn't lethal." One curious person shifts norms. One cynical person poisons the pool.

### The Expert Trap
More expertise can create more blind spots. The expert knows more reasons why the current CVS is correct. Self-deception is effortless — our brains seek confirmation of existing beliefs.

Feynman: *"The first principle is that you must not fool yourself — and you are the easiest person to fool."*

### The 2030 Divide
By 2030, the fault line between value fountains and value drains will be the ability to escape your own point of view — i.e., sustained curiosity under pressure.

AI is already doing logical thinking exponentially better than humans. **Lateral thinking is the human advantage.**
